FAQ

Can’t find your question in this list?
Reach out and we'll answer within two working days.

What kind of companies do you normally work with?

We typically partner with startups(Seed to Series B) facing a common challenge: they have an excellent product, but their website is holding them back. What brought them this far no longer works, and they're ready to align their brand and web presence with their products' reality and ambitions.
If you also have a great product that’s outpaced its perception, let’s have a call and explore how we can fix that.

How long does a project take, on average?

Projects typically run from one to four months. We know startups need to move fast to capitalize on their advantage, and our process is built to support that speed.

What's the typical price range?

Prices vary depending on the scope of work, but our minimum level of engagement is $4,900. To get a more accurate estimate, reach out to schedule a call to get a better sense of the scope of work, timeline and budget needed.

What are the payment terms?

The payment terms are typically 50% upfront, and 50% after we launch in impeccable working condition. For more complex projects, we can split the budget into more than two installments, depending on the scope of work.

What happens after we launch the website?

We'll be 100% hands on post-launch, making sure everything looks great and works impeccably. We'll be available for 30 days for any necessary fixes, as part of the original scope. Within the first week, we will onboard you to the Webflow CMS Editor, and offer training session so that you and your team can update the new website with ease.
And if you'll want to make improvements in the future, we're just one click away .

Will this process be time consuming for me and my team?

Our process requires minimal effort from you and your team. We will need your input mainly during the initial strategy workshops and during regular check-ins for feedback.

How do I know that you’ll deliver what we need?

All the specs and deliverables are clearly outlined in the project proposal. At the end of the project, before the final payment, we will make sure that everything is in line with the proposal we agreed upon.

Will this integrate with my existing marketing strategies?

Our solutions work seamlessly with your martech stack, complementing and elevating your existing workflows.

Will my team be able to update content of the website?

Absolutely! Webflow has an excellent CMS Editor, which allows members of your team to make timely updates safely. At the end of the project you'll receive a dedicated training video, in which we'll review the Editor and coach your team on how to use it.

Can you help us with brand strategy and positioning?

Yes, we do! We offer strategic support to refine your positioning and develop a comprehensive Brand Strategy Roadmap. This document serves as a foundation for your future marketing efforts and is available as an add-on service. Check out our case studies to learn more.

Do you offer brand identity, or logo design?

Absolutely! We can help bring your brand to life with a custom logo and a full identity system. It’s a great way to make sure your visuals match your strategy, and it's available as an optional upgrade. To see how we’ve helped other clients transform their brands, check out our case studies .

Can you migrate my website from WordPress, Wix or Squarespace?

Yes, we can make that transition. We'll maintain your URL structure intact, to preserve traffic and ranking.

How do we get started?

Please pick a time that works for you for our discovery call. We'll discuss your product, goals, and challenges, and see if there's an opportunity to work together.
