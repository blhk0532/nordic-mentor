Making cryptos easily accessible to the Everyman

Start2Coin is a physical card and online service that facilitates quick and easy access to Bitcoin in busy POS locations across Europe.

About
Start2Coin

SweePay launched Start2Coin with an audacious goal: to emocratize crypto access through a safe, easy-to-use and accessible physical card. This product was destined for non-tech-savvy users, who, while interested in the upside of crypto, lacked the confidence to invest.

The Need

SweePay wanted to be part of the solution, and bring cryptocurrencies into the mainstream and the pockets of the average Europeans. Another objective was to clear some of the misconceptions around this emerging technology, and provide a space where users can learn, transact and profit from Bitcoin.

Brand
Strategy

We began with a series of facilitated workshops, meant to gain a deeper understanding of the product, customer, market position, competition, and potential gaps and opportunities. We then delved into defining the brand attributes, and honed in on the desired look and feel.

Identity
System

The logo, a golden coin set in motion, perfectly framed in the brand initial letter is a reflection of the brand’s core mission: making safe and fast crypto trading accessible to everyone. The warm, welcoming color palette challenges the limiting belief that you have to be an expert to enjoy the benefits of Bitcoin.

Applications

The Start2Coin Card is a confident embodiment of the service: a fast, reliable, and modern tool for trading Bitcoin safely and easily. The brand identity is bold and scalable, seamlessly translating across the digital medium and branded merchandise.

Brand
Styleguide

The Website

The website positioned Start2Coin firmly on the market, and it served as a platform for new customers to activate their cards and get support, or for Bitcoin wallet holders to connect their accounts. The quick and easy activation process provides a seamless user experience that's true to the brand and is focused on the customer.

The Impact

Post-launch, the Start2Coin card has been released in thousands of locations across Europe. The brand has gained substantial recognition, featuring in notable publications like Yahoo! Finance, marking a significant stride in their mission to mainstream cryptocurrency usage.

Creative and well-structured process

The roadmap, milestones and overall budget were well defined from the beginning of the collaboration and at the end of the process we had a new perspective on the identity, personality and the purpose of our brand.

Rodolphe Texier

CEO of SweePay AG
